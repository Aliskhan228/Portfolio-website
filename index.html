Posters for the Animation Festival

Year: 2022

Overview

“Insomnia is a truly independent animation festival. It takes place every year in the big and beautiful field surrounded by forest and river“. I wanted to make posters for this festival after I went to it and was inspired by the people there.

Approache

I didn't have any suitable photos for the poster. But at this time, AI system DALE 2 became very popular. The queue for using the system was too big and I found an analogue. The photos turned out to be very blurry and interesting. After several manipulations in Photoshop, quite attractive photos turned out, which perfectly reflect how I perceived the festival. Very interesting experience of using AI system!

Identity for a photo studio

Client: Farsh Production

Year: 2022

Overview

The Kinofarsh production team of like-minded people has been creating video content of various genres and levels of complexity for more than 15 years, from videos and clips for YouTube to advertising and feature films for television. They decided to expand their audience and they needed their own style.

Challange

This was my first experience when I did all the identity for the company. It was decided to take a new name for the brand — “Farsh production”. The client had a request to link the field of filmmaking and stuffing (in Russian it will be “farsh”). Below you can see my developments.

Approache

I did an analysis of the target audience. I looked at the designs of competitors, took into account their mistakes and started working. I made a lot of logo options. As a result, the customer confirmed the logo version, where the letter R seems to spread like minced meat. Also in this you can see the film.

When I was making the pattern, I decided that I wanted to show the versatility of the company. The company can be serious, which shows sharp corners, and can be creative, which shows smooth lines. After that, I put together a brandbook, where I showed how to work with style, paid a lot of attention to typography. With the help of a brandbook, I created business cards and leaflets.

Results

As a result, the customer was happy. He really liked the pattern. I'm going to continue to keep in touch with the company and help them with the design.

Overview

Product task in glasses store

Year: 2022

Overview

I had a task to raise revenue in the eyewear store. I was given a funnel of visitors, a custom script, user portraits and given freedom of action.

Approache

What the problem was could be understood at a glance at the funnel. Users reached the product card, but did not add it to the cart. And there was also a difficulty with registration.

After studying the user scenario and user portraits, I began to write out hypotheses, which I will then test in in-depth interviews. Basically, all the barriers of users were reduced to the lack of a profile, to uncertainty in the choice of goods and to issues with delivery. Also, all users wanted to make purchases as quickly as possible without wasting time on unnecessary actions. Therefore, my decision was:

To test my hypotheses, I conducted three in-depth interviews with people very similar to user portraits. Having written the interview plan and questions, I conducted quite successful interviews in ZOOM. Starting with the warm-up questions and ending with more complex questions about drivers and barriers, I got all the information I needed. As a result, all my hypotheses were confirmed except for one. None of the respondents found the section with articles exciting. Therefore, I decided not to include the articles in the backlog. I chose three hypotheses that most influenced the conversion, in my opinion, and started working with them. Changing the product card. Here I added a doctor's consultation button and reviews from real buyers.

I changed the registration and added the possibility of fast delivery within 2-3 hours. Now, if you downloaded the app for the first time, but you already have an account, you do not need to fill out the registration form again. By clicking on the inscription “I already have an account” you go to the screen where you need to enter your email and password, and after that your addresses load themselves. If the customer makes an order for the first time, he no longer needs to fill in large registration fields, it is enough to specify the name, phone number and email. I also reduced the number of screens. Now registration, delivery selection and payment selection are on the same screen and the user now needs to do fewer actions.

Results

I hope that if this were a real grocery task, then my solution would increase the conversion of the basket by several tens of percent. But so far it has not been a real task. But the interviews and the redesign were real! The result of this case can be considered my new interviewing skill and a little more experience in analyzing the source data.

Digitalize the old paper form

Year: 2022

Overview

I had a passport application form from the fictional Hobbit world. I needed to convert this form to electronic form.

Challange

The problem with the paper form was the size restrictions. All the data had to fit on both sides of the A5 paper, which led to the fact that the applicant was confused in the sequence of fields and could have missed something. There was also an issue in numerous fields that had to be filled not by the applicant, but by a civil servant. Therefore, hobbits probably often did not understand what they needed to fill in. It was also not always clear what exactly should be written in this or that field.

There are no such difficulties in electronic form, I am not limited in space and can only provide the necessary fields. It was also necessary to make the most understandable form that any hobbit could fill.

Approache

I decided to divide the information into several blocks.

I also created drop-down boxes instead for some fields. I further created hints for some fields that may cause confusion among users.

In the paper form, there was a problem with the fields which did not apply to some respondents. For example, on the presence of marriage or children. I solved this problem and didn't force hobbits to fill in unnecessary fields. Now the form changes depending on the applicant's answers.

As a result, I got a small UI kit.

By the way, I also made a nice download!

Results

I really enjoyed doing research for new rules of the game, making them more balanced. It was a very interesting analysis and modeling experience. Also a nice moment is that I became a prize-winner of the competition “Vysshaya proba”.
